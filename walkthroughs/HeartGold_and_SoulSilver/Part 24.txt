Clefairy Dance
From 8:00 PM Monday through 4:00 AM Tuesday, six
Clefairy
appear in the field to the west. Four of them begin dancing around the pond, which shows a reflection of the full moon. When you enter the field, they get startled and run away, leaving behind an item that turns out to be a
Moon Stone
. The group returns to worship the moon every week.
Enter the south side of the cave for a shortcut to the exit.
Route 4
Route 4
Route 4
leads down from the mountains and eastward to
Cerulean City
. Collect the items, then fly back to
Pewter City
and head south.
