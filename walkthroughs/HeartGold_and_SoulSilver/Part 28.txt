Route 22

leads from
Viridian City
back to the

and

on foot, without taking the
S.S. Aqua
or the
Magnet Train

Route 28
Route 28
Route 28
is a hidden mountain road that leads to
Mt. Silver
. This is the final route in
Kanto
, and halfway to the mountain the path crosses back into
Johto
.

The Recluse
Cut down a small tree to reach the secluded house on the hill. The woman inside is a bit of a celebrity, and gives you
TM47
(
Steel Wing
) to keep her hideaway a secret.
Mt. Silver
Mt. Silver (exterior)
The road from
Route 28
leads directly to the Pokémon Center at the foot of the mountain. Make any last-minute preparations;
Surf
,
Waterfall
, and
Rock Climb
are required to reach every chamber, while
Rock Smash
is also recommended.
