Concise walkthrough:
- Route 3: Battle trainers, find hidden Ultra Ball and Super Potion.
- Wellspring Cave: Defeat Team Plasma Grunts, find TM46 (Thief).
- Nacrene City: Receive Chesto Berries from Cheren, get a power-up item based on starter choice.
- Nacrene Gym: Solve library puzzle, defeat Lenora for Basic Badge and TM67 (Retaliate).
- Follow Team Plasma to museum, meet Burgh and receive Dowsing MCHN from Bianca.
- Head to Pinwheel Forest with Burgh.
