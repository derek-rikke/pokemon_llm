Indigo Plateau
The Man, The Myth, The Legend
Another legendary Trainer can be found outside the
Pokémon League
building at
Indigo Plateau
. To trigger
Red
's appearance, you must defeat six of the
Master Trainers
scattered across
Kanto
. After doing so, use
Sky Dash
to return to the building's main entrance; Red can be seen to the west, beyond the row of pillars. Approach him with a full team of six Pokémon and he readies a Poké Ball; you are then given the option to battle. Red battles under the same conditions as the Master Trainers, so using items is prohibited. Choosing to run will forfeit the battle, but losing does not result in losing any money. Defeating him earns you the title of
Battle Master
, which may be displayed in link battles against other Trainers.
