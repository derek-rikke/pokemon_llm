### Part 29: Survival Area, Route 226, Route 227, Stark Mountain

1. **Survival Area**: 
   - Heal your Pokémon at the Pokémon Center.
   - Stock up on healing items and Poké Balls.

2. **Route 226**: 
   - Navigate through the grassy areas and battle trainers.
   - Look for hidden items like Rare Candies.
   - Head north to reach Route 227.

3. **Route 227**: 
   - Prepare for battles with trainers and wild Pokémon.
   - Use Surf to cross water patches.
   - Collect items such as Max Potions and a TM.

4. **Stark Mountain**: 
   - Enter the cave and battle Team Galactic.
   - Use Rock Smash to clear boulders.
   - Defeat the Galactic Grunts and reach the summit.
   - Encounter and catch the Legendary Pokémon Heatran.

5. **Strategies**:
   - Keep a strong Fire-type or Fighting-type Pokémon for battles.
   - Stock up on Revives and Potions for challenging fights.
   - Save before key encounters, especially with Legendary Pokémon.

Follow these concise steps to navigate through the Survival Area, Route 226, Route 227, and Stark Mountain effectively.
