**Part 12 Walkthrough: Route 212, Route 210 (North), Celestic Town, Route 211 (East), Hearthome Gym**

1. **Route 212**: 
   - Traverse through grass and battles. 
   - Defeat trainers to gain experience.
   - Look for items like the Big Pearl and the TM for Water Pulse.

2. **Route 210 (North)**: 
   - Head north from Route 212. 
   - Use Cut on the trees to navigate. 
   - Prepare for battles against trainers and wild Pokémon.

3. **Celestic Town**: 
   - Visit the local Pokémon Center to heal.
   - Explore for items, including the HM for Surf.
   - Talk to the NPCs for lore and quest hints.

4. **Route 211 (East)**: 
   - Proceed east from Celestic Town. 
   - Defeat trainers and catch Pokémon.
   - Find the TM for Rock Slide along the way.

5. **Hearthome Gym**: 
   - Head to Hearthome City.
   - Challenge the Gym Leader, Fantina, who specializes in Ghost-type Pokémon.
   - Use Dark or Ghost-type moves to gain an advantage in battle.

Remember to stock up on healing items and status cures before challenging the gym. Good luck!
