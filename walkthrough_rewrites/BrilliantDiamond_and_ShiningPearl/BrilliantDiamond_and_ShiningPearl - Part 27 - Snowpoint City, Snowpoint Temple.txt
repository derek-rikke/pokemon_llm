**Part 27: Snowpoint City and Snowpoint Temple**

1. **Snowpoint City**: 
   - Heal your Pokémon at the Pokémon Center.
   - Stock up on items at the Poké Mart.

2. **Snowpoint Temple**:
   - Head north from Snowpoint City to reach the temple entrance.
   - Inside, navigate through the temple by solving ice puzzles and battling trainers.
   - Key items to collect:
     - **HM07 (Waterfall)**: Required to progress.
     - **Rare Candies** and **TM** items scattered throughout.

3. **Battle Strategy**:
   - Prepare for battles against Ice-type Pokémon.
   - Use Fire, Steel, Rock, or Fighting-type moves for an advantage.

4. **Key Challenges**:
   - Defeat the temple's guardian to unlock the way forward.
   - Be ready for a tough battle against the Legendary Pokémon at the end.

5. **Exit**: 
   - After completing the temple, exit and continue your journey to the next destination. 

Ensure your team is well-prepared and stocked with healing items for optimal progress.
