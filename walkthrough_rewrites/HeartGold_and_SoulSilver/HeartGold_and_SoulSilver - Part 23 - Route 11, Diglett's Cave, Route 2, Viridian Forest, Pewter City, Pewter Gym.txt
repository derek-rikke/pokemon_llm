Keywords: Route 11, Diglett's Cave, Route 2, Viridian Forest, Pewter City, Pewter Gym

**Part 23: Route 11 to Pewter City**

**Route 11**
- Connects Route 12 to Vermilion City. 
- To wake Snorlax, tune your Pokégear’s radio to the Poké Flute Channel and approach it for battle. After defeating it, Diglett's Cave will be accessible.
- **Trainers**:
  - Psychic Herman: Exeggcute (Lv. 39) x2, Exeggutor (Lv. 39) - Reward: $1248
  - Youngster Jason: Sandslash (Lv. 42), Crobat (Lv. 42) - Reward: $672
  - Youngster Owen: Growlithe (Lv. 44) - Reward: $704
  - Psychic Fidel: Xatu (Lv. 43) - Reward: $1376

- **Items**:
  - Green Apricorn: Center tree
  - Revive: Hidden on the north side
  - TM86 (Grass Knot): Northeast corner

**Diglett's Cave**
- Connects Route 11 with Route 2.
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Diglett (Lv. 13-19, 90%), Dugtrio (Lv. 19-29, 10%)
- **Items**:
  - Max Revive: Hidden on lower level
  - Calcium: Hidden on upper level

**Route 2**
- Connects Viridian City and Pewter City.
- **Trainers**:
  - Bug Catcher Ed: Burmy (Lv. 43), Beedrill (Lv. 43), Butterfree (Lv. 43) - Reward: $688
  - Bug Catcher Doug: Butterfree (Lv. 39), Ariados (Lv. 37) - Reward: $592

- **Items**:
  - Carbos: Northeast of Diglett's Cave
  - Pink Apricorn: South of Diglett's Cave
  - Nugget: From a man in the first house south of Diglett's Cave

**Viridian Forest**
- Wild Pikachu can be found here.
- **Trainers**:
  - Bug Catcher Abner: Butterfree (Lv. 44), Beedrill (Lv. 44), Pikachu (Lv. 44) - Reward: $704

- **Items**:
  - Max Ether: Hidden in the southwest corner (requires Cut)
  - Leaf Stone: Southwest part

**Pewter City**
- Home to the Pewter Museum of Science and Pewter Gym.
- **Items**:
  - Max Revive: Near Route 2 exit (hidden)
  - Silver Wing: Given by an old man on the east side
  - TM80 (Rock Slide): From Brock after defeating him

**Pewter Gym**
- Leader: Brock (Rock/Ground-type).
- Use Grass and Water-type moves for effectiveness.
- **Brock's Pokémon**:
  - Graveler (Lv. 51), Rhyhorn (Lv. 51), Omastar (Lv. 53), Onix (Lv. 54), Kabutops (Lv. 52)
- Defeating Brock earns the Boulder Badge and TM80 (Rock Slide).

**Trade Opportunity**
- Inside the Pokémon Center, trade a Haunter for Xatu.

**Legendary Pokémon**
- Speak to an old man on a hill for the Silver Wing (HG) or Rainbow Wing (SS) to hunt Lugia or Ho-Oh.

Continue to explore or head to the next part of your journey!
