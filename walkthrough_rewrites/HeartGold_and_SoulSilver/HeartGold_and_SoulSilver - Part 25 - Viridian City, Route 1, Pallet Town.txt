**Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver Walkthrough: Part 25**

**Viridian City**
- **Overview**: Small city in Kanto. The Viridian Gym is currently closed. 
- **Trainer House**: Battle against Ace Trainer Cal (uses Johto starters). Win for Battle Points (BP).
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Surfing: Poliwag (5-10), Poliwhirl (10)
  - Fishing: Magikarp (10), Poliwag (10/20/40)
  - Headbutt: Hoothoot (3-7), Spinarak (5-7), Ledyba (5-7), Pineco (3-4), Wurmple (3-7)
- **Items**: 
  - Nugget (hidden, northeast of Poké Mart)
  - TM85 (Dream Eater) - on ridge (requires Cut/Surf)
  - TM92 (Trick Room) - from Blue after defeating him.

**Route 1**
- **Trainers**: 
  - Sherman (Furret, Pidgeot) - $860
  - Danny (Jynx, Electabuzz, Magmar) - $860
  - Ace Trainer French (Absol, Alakazam) - $2820
  - Ace Trainer Quinn (Ivysaur, Starmie) - $2820
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Grass: Pidgey (2-4), Rattata (2), Sentret (3), Furret (6)
  - Headbutt: Hoothoot (2-3), Spinarak (5-6), Ledyba (5-6)
- **Items**: Blk Apricorn (northwest hill)

**Pallet Town**
- **Overview**: Home of former champions Red and Blue. 
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Surfing: Tentacool (30-35), Fishing: Magikarp (10)
  - Gift Pokémon: Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle (all level 5)
- **Items**: 
  - HM08 (Rock Climb) - from Professor Oak after earning all 16 Badges.
  - Jade Orb - from Oak after showing both Groudon and Kyogre.

**Locations**: 
- **Red's House**: Red is absent, but his mother is available.
- **Blue's House**: Daisy grooms Pokémon daily (3 PM - 4 PM).
- **Professor Oak's Lab**: Speak to Oak for a gift after defeating all Kanto Gym Leaders. 

This guide outlines key battles, available Pokémon, and important items in Viridian City, Route 1, and Pallet Town. Focus on defeating trainers and collecting items for progression.
