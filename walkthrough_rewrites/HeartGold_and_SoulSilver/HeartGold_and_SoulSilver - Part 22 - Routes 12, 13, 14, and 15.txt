Game: HeartGold_and_SoulSilver

**Walkthrough: Part 22 - Routes 12, 13, 14, and 15**

**Route 15**
- **Key Items:** 
  - PP Up (East side, cut tree)
  - Rose Incense (Southern path)
  
- **Trainers:**
  - School Kid Kipp: Voltorb (Lv. 36, 40), Magneton (Lv. 40) - $800
  - School Kid Tommy: Xatu (Lv. 41), Alakazam (Lv. 43) - $860
  - Teacher Hillary: Sunkern (Lv. 41), Aipom (Lv. 31) - $1488
  - Pokéfan Boone: Spinda (Lv. 43), Volbeat (Lv. 41) - $2624
  - Trainer Colette: Clefairy (Lv. 45) - $2160

- **Available Pokémon:**
  - Nidorina, Nidorino (Lv. 23), Pidgeotto (Lv. 25), Chansey (Lv. 25, rare)

**Route 14**
- **Key Trainers:**
  - Bird Keeper Josh: Fearow (Lv. 37) - $1184
  - Pokéfan Trevor: Psyduck (Lv. 42) - $2688
  - Teacher Clarice: Zigzagoon (Lv. 41), Roselia (Lv. 43) - $2064

- **Available Pokémon:**
  - Nidorina, Nidorino (Lv. 23), Hoppip (Lv. 24), Chansey (Lv. 25, rare)

- **Item:** Lucky Punch (From woman in tall grass with Chansey)

**Route 13**
- **Key Trainers:**
  - Bird Keeper Bret: Taillow (Lv. 41) - $1312
  - Young Couple Tim & Sue: Kadabra (Lv. 41), Psyduck (Lv. 46) - $5888

- **Available Pokémon:**
  - Nidorina, Nidorino (Lv. 23), Tentacool (Surfing), Magikarp (Fishing)

- **Items:** Calcium (Hidden East of Trainer Tips sign)

**Route 12**
- **Key Trainers:**
  - Fisherman Kyle: Goldeen (Lv. 39), Qwilfish (Lv. 35) - $1248
  - Bird Keeper Justin: Xatu (Lv. 44) - $1248

- **Available Pokémon:**
  - Tentacruel (Surfing), Quagsire (Fishing)

- **Items:**
  - Yellow Flute (Southwest field, requires Surf/Cut)
  - Super Rod (From Fishing Guru's brother)

**Final Sighting**  
- Encounter Suicune again while heading north. After this, you'll be able to challenge it at Cerulean Cape.

Make sure to battle trainers for experience and gather items as you progress through these routes!
