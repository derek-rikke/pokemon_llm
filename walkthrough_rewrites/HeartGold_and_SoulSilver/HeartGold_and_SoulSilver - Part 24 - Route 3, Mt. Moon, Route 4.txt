### Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver Walkthrough: Part 24

**Route 3**
- Connects Pewter City to Mt. Moon. 
- **Trainers**:
  - *Youngster Regis*: Golbat (Lv. 40), Electrode (Lv. 40) - Reward: $640
  - *Youngster Warren*: Fearow (Lv. 38), Raticate (Lv. 42) - Reward: $672
  - *Double Team (Zac & Jen)*: Dugtrio (Lv. 47), Electabuzz (Lv. 47) - Reward: $11,280
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Rattata (5-10), Spearow (5-8), Ekans (8), Jigglypuff (6), Zubat (5)
- **Items**:
  - Big Root (south of Hiker Bruce)
  - Hyper Potion (hidden, west of Pokémon Center)
  - Star Pieces (inside craters)

**Mt. Moon**
- Single chamber connects Route 3 to Route 4. 
- Encounter **Silver** who challenges you.
- **Available Pokémon**:
  - Zubat (6), Geodude (6-10), Sandshrew (8), Paras (12)
- **Items**:
  - Revive (hidden rock)

**Mt. Moon Square**
- Clefairy dance occurs Monday nights.
- **Items**:
  - Max Revive (east of trees)
  - Moon Stone (near pond during Clefairy event)
- **Shop**: Sells items like Fresh Water ($200), Lemonade ($350), Repel ($350).

**Route 4**
- Leads to Cerulean City.
- **Trainers**:
  - *Picnicker Hope*: Flaaffy (Lv. 44) - Reward: $704
  - *Bird Keeper Hank*: Pidgeot (Lv. 44) - Reward: $1,408
- **Available Pokémon**:
  - Ekans (8), Jigglypuff (6), Spearow (5-8)
- **Items**:
  - Big Mushroom (southeast of Bird Keeper Hank)
  - HP Up (ledge to the east)
  - Ultra Ball (northeast of Picnicker Hope)

### Summary
Focus on battling trainers for experience and collecting items throughout Route 3, Mt. Moon, and Route 4. Participate in the Clefairy dance to obtain a Moon Stone and continue your journey toward Cerulean City.
