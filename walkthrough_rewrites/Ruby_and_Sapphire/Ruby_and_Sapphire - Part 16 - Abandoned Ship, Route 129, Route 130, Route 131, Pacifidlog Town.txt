## Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire Walkthrough: Part 16

### Key Locations: Abandoned Ship, Route 129, Route 130, Route 131, Pacifidlog Town

### Abandoned Ship (Route 108)
- **Access**: Use Surf and Dive.
- **Trainers**:
  - B1F: Sailor Duncan (Wailmer, Machoke).
  - 1F: Tuber Charlie (Marill), Young Couple Lois & Hal (Volbeat, Illumise).
- **Items**:
  - 1F: Harbor Mail (northwest room), Revive (north room).
  - B1F: Dive Ball (north-central cabin), Escape Rope (southwest cabin), TM13 (Ice Beam, requires Storage Key).
  - **Hidden Items**: Storage Key (Captain's Office), Rm. 1 Key (Room 3), Water Stone (Room 3), TM18 (Rain Dance, Room 1), Scanner (Room 2).
- **Objective**: Retrieve the Scanner for Captain Stern in Slateport Harbor, trade it for either DeepSeaTooth or DeepSeaScale.

### Route 129
- **Trainers**:
  - Triathlete Allison (Staryu), Triathlete Chase (Staryu), Swimmer Reed (Carvanha, Sharpedo).
- **Pokémon**: Surfing brings Tentacool, Wingull, Wailord; fishing yields Magikarp and Tentacool.

### Route 130
- **Trainers**:
  - Swimmer Katie (Goldeen, Chinchou, Spheal), Swimmer Rodney (Horsea, Gyarados).
- **Mirage Island**: Occasionally appears, home to Wynaut (Lv. 5-50) and a Liechi Berry tree.

### Route 131
- **Trainers**:
  - Swimmer Kara (Seaking), Swimmer Herman (Wailmer, Tentacruel), Swimmer Susie (Horsea, Wailmer).
- **Pokémon**: Similar to previous routes - Tentacool, Wingull, Wailmer with fishing yields similar results.

### Pacifidlog Town
- **Overview**: Floating village with a Pokémon Center.
- **Trainers**: Trade Corsola for Bellossom with a local.
- **Items**: TM21 (Frustration) or TM27 (Return) based on friendship with lead Pokémon.
- **Legends**: Speak to locals for hints about the region's mysteries.

### Important Notes:
- Rematches available with trainers having a PokéNav.
- Collect items before proceeding to the next area to enhance your journey.
