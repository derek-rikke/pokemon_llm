**Part 6: Route 6, Vermilion City, S.S. Anne, Vermilion Gym**

**Route 6:** 
- Travel south from Saffron City to reach Vermilion City.
- Key Trainers:
  - Bug Catcher: 3x Weedle (Lv. 16), Caterpie (Lv. 16) - Reward: $160
  - Jr. Trainer♂: Squirtle (Lv. 20) - Reward: $400
  - Bug Catcher: Butterfree (Lv. 20) - Reward: $200

**Available Pokémon:**
- Oddish (Lv. 13, 15-16, 40%), Bellsprout (Lv. 13, 15-16, 40%), Meowth (Lv. 10, 12, 14, 16, 25%)

**Vermilion City:**
- **Fishing Guru:** Get Old Rod from the house in the northwest.
- **Trade:** Exchange Spearow for Farfetch'd in the central house.
- **Pokémon Fan Club:** Receive a Bike Voucher from the Chairman.

**Vermilion Harbor & S.S. Anne:**
- **S.S. Anne:** 
  - 1F: Key Trainers include Gentlemen and Sailors with levels 17-23.
  - B1F: Collect items (Max Potion, Ether, TM44).
  - 2F: Obtain Max Ether and Rare Candy. 
  - **Rival Battle:** 
    - Pidgeotto (Lv. 19), Raticate (Lv. 16), Kadabra (Lv. 18). Use Electric and Rock moves for best results. 

- **Items on S.S. Anne:**
  - TM08 (Body Slam), Max Ether, Rare Candy, HM01 (Cut) from the Captain after helping him.

**Vermilion Gym:**
- **Leader:** Lt. Surge (Electric-type).
- **Strategy:** Use Ground-type Pokémon against his Electric-types (Voltorb, Pikachu, Raichu).
- **Puzzle:** Flip switches hidden in trash cans to access the leader.
- **Rewards:** Thunder Badge and TM24 (Thunderbolt).

**Next Destination:** Route 11 to the east.
