**Part 12 Walkthrough: Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon**

1. **Memorial Hill**: 
   - After defeating the previous trial, head to Memorial Hill.
   - Battle trainers and catch Pokémon in the area.
   - Find items such as Revives and Potions.

2. **Akala Outskirts**: 
   - Continue to the Akala Outskirts.
   - Look for TM items and additional trainers to battle.
   - Stock up on healing items before proceeding.

3. **Ruins of Life**: 
   - Make your way to the Ruins of Life.
   - Prepare for the Grand Trial against Lana, focusing on Water-type Pokémon.
   - Utilize Electric-type moves against her Pokémon.

4. **Akala Grand Trial**: 
   - After defeating Lana, you’ll face the Akala Grand Trial.
   - Ensure your team is leveled and equipped with Potions.
   - Use strong Fighting-type moves against the Trial Captain.

5. **Hano Grand Resort**: 
   - Post-trial, visit the Hano Grand Resort.
   - Interact with NPCs for side quests and items.
   - Heal your Pokémon and prepare for upcoming challenges.

6. **Hano Beach**: 
   - Explore Hano Beach for new Pokémon and trainers.
   - Collect items like Berries and TMs.
   - Engage in battles to strengthen your team further.

**Key Tips**:
- Always keep your Pokémon healed and stocked with items.
- Focus on type advantages in battles.
- Explore thoroughly for hidden items and trainers.
