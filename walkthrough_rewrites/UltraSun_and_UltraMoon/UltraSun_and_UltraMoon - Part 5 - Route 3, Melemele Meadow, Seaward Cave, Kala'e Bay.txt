**Part 5 Walkthrough: Route 3, Melemele Meadow, Seaward Cave, Kala'e Bay**

1. **Route 3**: 
   - Head south from Hau'oli City. 
   - Battle trainers and capture Pokémon in the tall grass.
   - Look for items like Potions and Revives.

2. **Melemele Meadow**: 
   - Enter the meadow to find wild Pokémon and trainers.
   - Collect items such as Honey and a TM (if available).
   - Complete the trial by defeating the Totem Pokémon.

3. **Seaward Cave**: 
   - Access the cave on the northwest side of Route 3.
   - Capture Water and Rock-type Pokémon.
   - Look for items like Repels and Poké Balls.

4. **Kala'e Bay**: 
   - Surf to reach Kala'e Bay.
   - Battle trainers and find rare Pokémon.
   - Collect items such as a Rare Candy or a fishing rod.

**Key Strategies**:
- Use type advantages in battles (e.g., Water against Fire).
- Stock up on healing items and Poké Balls before major battles.
- Explore all areas thoroughly for hidden items and Pokémon. 

This walkthrough provides key locations and strategies to progress through the game efficiently.
