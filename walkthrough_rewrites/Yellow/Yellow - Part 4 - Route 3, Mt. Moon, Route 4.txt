**Part 4: Route 3, Mt. Moon, Route 4 - Pokémon Yellow Walkthrough**

**Route 3**
- Proceed east from Pewter City.
- Battle Trainers for experience and cash:
  - **Lass**: Pidgey (Lv. 9) x2, Reward: $135
  - **Bug Catcher**: Caterpie (Lv. 10) x3, Reward: $100
  - **Youngster**: Rattata (Lv. 11), Ekans (Lv. 11), Reward: $165

**Available Pokémon**: 
- Spearow (Lv. 8-12), Rattata (Lv. 10, 12), Sandshrew (Lv. 8, 10), Mankey (Lv. 9).

**Route 4 (West)**
- Visit the Pokémon Center at the entrance to Mt. Moon.
- **Magikarp Salesman**: Buy a Magikarp for $500.

**Mt. Moon**
1. **1F**:
   - Collect **TM12 (Water Gun)**, **Potion** x2, **Rare Candy**, **Escape Rope**, **Moon Stone**.
   - Trainers: 
     - **Lass**: Clefairy (Lv. 14), Reward: $210
     - **Super Nerd**: Magnemite (Lv. 11), Reward: $275
2. **B2F (South)**:
   - Battle Team Rocket Grunts, collect **HP Up**.
3. **B2F (Northeast)**:
   - Fight Team Rocket for **TM01 (Mega Punch)** and find a hidden **Ether**.
4. **B2F (Outer Area)**:
   - Defeat the Super Nerd for a choice of **Dome Fossil** (Kabuto) or **Helix Fossil** (Omanyte).
   - Battle two Team Rocket Grunts before exiting.

**Available Pokémon**:
- **1F**: Zubat (Lv. 6-11), Geodude (Lv. 10), Clefairy (Lv. 11).
- **B1F**: Zubat (Lv. 8-11), Paras (Lv. 9, 11).
- **B2F**: Zubat (Lv. 10-13), Paras (Lv. 13).

**Items**:
- **1F**: TM12, Potions, Rare Candy, Escape Rope, Moon Stone.
- **B2F**: HP Up, TM01, hidden Ether, Moon Stones, Fossils.

**Route 4 (East)**
- Leads downhill to Cerulean City.
- **Available Pokémon**: Spearow (Lv. 8-12), Rattata (Lv. 10, 12), Sandshrew (Lv. 8, 10), Mankey (Lv. 9).
- **Items**: Great Ball (hidden), TM04 (Whirlwind).

**Next Steps**: Progress to Cerulean City and prepare for upcoming challenges.
