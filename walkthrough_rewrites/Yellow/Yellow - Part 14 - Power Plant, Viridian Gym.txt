### Pokémon Yellow Walkthrough: Part 14 - Power Plant and Viridian Gym

#### Power Plant
1. **Location:** Industrial complex with hidden items and Pokémon.
2. **Key Pokémon:**
   - **Magnemite:** Levels 30-35 (40%)
   - **Magneton:** Levels 33-38 (20%)
   - **Grimer:** Levels 33-37 (15%)
   - **Muk:** Levels 33-37 (5%)
   - **Voltorb:** Levels 33-37 (20%)
   - **Zapdos:** Level 50 (1x encounter).

3. **Important Items:**
   - **Carbos:** North of entrance.
   - **TM33 (Reflect):** South-central room.
   - **TM25 (Thunder):** Southeast room.
   - **Rare Candy & HP Up:** Northeast room.
   - **PP Up:** Hidden in rubble northeast of Zapdos.
   - **Max Elixir:** Hidden in central room.

4. **Strategy for Zapdos:**
   - Use Rock- and Ice-type moves, then Grass, Flying, and Fighting to weaken it.
   - Inflict Sleep status to increase catch chances.

#### Viridian City
1. **Gym Information:**
   - **Leader:** Giovanni (Ground-type Pokémon).
   - **Recommended Types:** Grass, Water, Ice, Flying, Psychic.
   
2. **Gym Layout:**
   - Go past three trainers to find a Revive.
   - Battle trainers and navigate spinner tiles to reach Giovanni.
   
3. **Giovanni's Pokémon:**
   - **Dugtrio (Lv. 50):** Use Water and Grass attacks.
   - **Persian (Lv. 53):** Normal-type.
   - **Nidoqueen (Lv. 53):** Poison/Ground-type.
   - **Nidoking (Lv. 55):** Poison/Ground-type.
   - **Rhydon (Lv. 55):** Ground/Rock-type.

4. **Post-Battle:**
   - Defeating Giovanni awards the **Earth Badge** and **TM27 (Fissure)**.
   - Giovanni officially disbands Team Rocket.

#### Next Steps
- Head to **Indigo Plateau** to challenge the Elite Four.
- Prepare for **Victory Road**; ensure your team has **Surf** and **Strength**. Stock up on Potions and Poké Balls. 

### Summary
Complete the Power Plant for items and capture Zapdos, then defeat Giovanni in Viridian Gym to earn the Earth Badge before heading to the Indigo Plateau.
