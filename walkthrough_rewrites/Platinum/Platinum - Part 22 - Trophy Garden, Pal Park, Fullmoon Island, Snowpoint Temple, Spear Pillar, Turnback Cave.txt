Game: Platinum
Part: 22
Keywords: Trophy Garden, Pal Park, Fullmoon Island, Snowpoint Temple, Spear Pillar, Turnback Cave

**Concise Walkthrough: Part 22**

**Trophy Garden**
- **Available Pokémon**: Pikachu, Pichu, Roselia, Staravia, Kricketune, Clefairy, Jigglypuff, Chansey, Ditto, Eevee, etc. 
- **Special Pokémon**: Talk to Mr. Backlot (after getting the National Pokédex) for rare Pokémon daily.

**Pal Park**
- **Import Pokémon**: Use GBA games to migrate Pokémon from Hoenn and Kanto.
- **Key Items**: Kitchen Timer app (show Snorlax), Color Changer app (show Kecleon).
- **Regigigas**: Requires Regirock, Regice, and Registeel to catch.

**Canalave City**
- **Quest**: Speak to Sailor Eldritch for the Lunar Wing to save his son.

**Fullmoon Island**
- **Item**: Lunar Wing (after Cresselia flees).
- **Note**: Cresselia roams the Sinnoh region.

**Snowpoint Temple**
- **Required Pokémon**: Regirock, Regice, Registeel to access Regigigas.
- **Items**: Calcium (B1F), Full Restore (B3F), Full Heal (B4F).
- **Regigigas Battle**: Use False Swipe and Dusk/Nest Balls to catch it after weakening.

**Mt. Coronet (South)**
- **Items**: Adamant Orb, Lustrous Orb for Dialga and Palkia.
- **TM02**: Found in the north area.

**Celestic Town**
- **Contact Cynthia's Grandmother** for Dialga and Palkia battle.

**Spear Pillar**
- **Dialga and Palkia Battle**: Save before interacting with the rifts. Use paralysis/sleep tactics and appropriate Poké Balls (Net Balls for Palkia).

**Turnback Cave**
- **Objective**: Catch Giratina.
- **Preparation**: Bring about 40 Dusk Balls. Use Defog to clear fog.
- **Pillar Navigation**: Follow the pillar hints to find the portal to the Distortion World and Giratina.

**Giratina Battle**: 
- **Level**: 47, use the same tactics as with Azelf and Uxie. Save before the encounter.

This concise guide focuses on key locations, important items, and battle strategies for progressing through the game effectively.
