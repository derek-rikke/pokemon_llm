**Part 13: Iron Island and Lake Valor Walkthrough**

### Iron Island
1. **Outside Iron Island**
   - **Available Pokémon**: Tentacool, Tentacruel, Wingull, Pelipper (Surf), Magikarp, Finneon (Fishing).
   - **Items**: 
     - Star Piece (hidden rock outside cave).
     - HM04 (Strength from Riley).
     - Metal Coat (house after National Dex).
   - **Tip**: Bring only five Pokémon for a Pokémon Egg later.

2. **1F**
   - **Available Pokémon**: Zubat, Golbat, Geodude, Graveler, Onix.
   - **Instructions**: Go down left stairs to B1F.

3. **B1F**
   - **Available Pokémon**: Golbat, Graveler, Onix, Sableye (Dual-slot), Mawile (Dual-slot).
   - **Items**: Protector, Yellow Shard, Escape Rope, Max Repel, Revive, Star Piece.
   - **Tip**: Collect items and face trainers; take right stairs to B2F.

4. **B2F**
   - **Available Pokémon**: Graveler, Steelix (rare), and gift Pokémon Riolu (Egg).
   - **Items**: Elixir, TM23 (Iron Tail), various hidden Star Pieces.
   - **Tip**: Team up with Riley for battles; he heals you after each fight.

5. **Final Area**
   - **Items**: Shiny Stone, Metal Coat, Nugget in Registeel's cave.
   - **Tip**: Use elevator to exit and return to Canalave City for the Mine Badge.

### Canalave Gym
1. **Overview**: Steel-type Gym with a maze layout.
   - **Strategy**: Use Fire, Ground, Fighting, and Special attacks against Steel Pokémon.
   - **Instructions**: Navigate through elevators to reach Byron on 4F.

2. **Byron's Team**:
   - Magneton (Lv. 37) - weak to Fire/Ground.
   - Steelix (Lv. 38) - avoid Earthquake with Fire-types.
   - Bastiodon (Lv. 41) - vulnerable to Fighting/Ground attacks.
   - **Reward**: Mine Badge and TM91 (Flash Cannon).

### Canalave City
- **Post-Gym**: Meet Barry at Canalave Library; discuss Lake guardians.
- **Next Objective**: Go to Lake Valor, where Team Galactic is causing trouble.

### Oreburgh Gate (Optional)
- **Items**: Earth Plate, TM01 (Focus Punch) accessible now with Strength.

### Lake Valor
- **Situation**: Team Galactic has dried up the lake using a bomb.
- **Battles**: Defeat Galactic Grunts to enter Valor Cavern and face Commander Saturn.
  - **Saturn's Team**:
    - Golbat (Lv. 38) - weak to Electric/Rock.
    - Bronzor (Lv. 38) - weak to Fire.
    - Toxicroak (Lv. 40) - weak to Psychic, Ground, and Flying.
- **Post-Battle**: Head to Lake Verity after defeating Saturn.

This concise guide captures essential instructions, items, and battle strategies for progressing through Iron Island, Canalave Gym, and Lake Valor.
