### Pokémon Gold and Silver Walkthrough: Part 23

#### Route 11
- **Location**: Between Route 12 and Vermilion City.
- **Trainers**: 
  - **Psychic Herman**: Exeggcute (Lv. 30) x2, Exeggutor (Lv. 30) - Reward: $960
  - **Youngster Owen**: Growlithe (Lv. 35) - Reward: $560
  - **Youngster Jason**: Sandslash (Lv. 33), Crobat (Lv. 33) - Reward: $528
  - **Psychic Fidel**: Xatu (Lv. 34) - Reward: $1088

- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Rattata (Lv. 15)
  - Magnemite (Lv. 15)
  - Drowzee (Lv. 14, 16)
  - Hypno (Lv. 16)

- **Items**: 
  - Berry: Northeast corner (daily)
  - Revive: Hidden, south of the Berry tree

#### Vermilion City
- **Roadblock**: Snorlax (Lv. 50) blocks the path. Use the Pokégear set to Channel 20.0 to wake it with the Poké Flute.

#### Diglett's Cave
- **Location**: Links Vermilion City to Route 2.
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Diglett (Lv. 13-19) - 90%
  - Dugtrio (Lv. 19-29) - 10%

- **Items**: 
  - Max Revive: Hidden near the north end

#### Route 2
- **Location**: Between Viridian City and Pewter City.
- **Trainers**: 
  - **Bug Catcher Rob**: Beedrill (Lv. 32) x2 - Reward: $512
  - **Bug Catcher Doug**: Ariados (Lv. 34) - Reward: $544

- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Caterpie (Lv. 3)
  - Weedle (Lv. 3)

- **Items**: 
  - Carbos: Northeast of Diglett's Cave entrance
  - Nugget: From a man in a house

- **A Friendly Face**: Speak to the man near Diglett's Cave for a Nugget.

#### Pewter City
- **Overview**: Features the Pewter Gym and the Pewter Museum of Science (closed for renovations).

- **Trading**: Trade a Gloom for a Rapidash at the Pokémon Center.

- **Items**: 
  - Burnt Berry: Held by traded Rapidash
  - Mint Berry: Northeast hill (daily)
  - Silver Wing/Rainbow Wing: From an old man near Route 3 exit

#### Pewter Gym
- **Leader**: Brock (Rock/Ground-type)
- **Strategy**: Use Grass and Water-type moves against his Pokémon.
  
- **Brock’s Pokémon**:
  - Graveler (Lv. 41), Rhyhorn (Lv. 41), Omastar (Lv. 42), Onix (Lv. 44), Kabutops (Lv. 42)

- **Reward**: Boulder Badge after defeating Brock.

- **Second Feather**: Speak to an elderly man near the east side for the Silver Wing (Gold/Silver) or Rainbow Wing (Silver).
