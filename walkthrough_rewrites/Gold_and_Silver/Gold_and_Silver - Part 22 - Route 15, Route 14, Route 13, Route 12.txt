Part: 22
Keywords: Route 15, Route 14, Route 13, Route 12

**Pokémon Gold and Silver Walkthrough - Part 22**

**Route 15**
- Hilly path from Fuchsia City to Route 14.
- **Trainers:** 
  - Schoolboy Kipp (Voltorb, Magnemite)
  - Schoolboy Tommy (Xatu, Alakazam)
  - Teacher Hillary (Aipom, Cubone)
  - Schoolboy Billy (Paras, Ditto)
  - Teacher Colette (Clefairy)
  - Schoolboy Johnny (Bellsprout, Victreebel)
- **Item:** PP Up (west side, requires Cut).
- **Available Pokémon:** Pidgeotto, Nidorina, Chansey, Hoppip, Quagsire.

**Route 14**
- Coastal road leading to Route 13.
- **Trainers:**
  - Bird Keeper Roy (Fearow)
  - Pokéfan Carter (Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle)
  - Pokéfan Trevor (Psyduck)
- **Special Trade:** Trade Chansey for Aerodactyl (requires Cut).
- **Items:** Gold Berry (held by Aerodactyl).

**Route 13**
- Narrow path with many trainers.
- **Trainers:**
  - Bird Keeper Bret (Pidgeotto, Fearow)
  - Bird Keeper Perry (Farfetch'd)
  - Pokéfan Joshua (Pikachu)
  - Pokéfan Alex (Nidoking, Slowking)
  - Hiker Kenny (Sandslash, Golem)
- **Items:** Hidden Calcium (east of Trainer Tips sign).
- **Available Pokémon:** Pidgeotto, Nidorina, Chansey, Tentacool (surfing).

**Route 12**
- Connects Route 13, Route 11, and Lavender Town.
- **Trainers:**
  - Fisher Barney (Gyarados)
  - Fisher Stephen (Magikarp, Qwilfish)
  - Fisher Martin (Remoraid)
  - Fisher Kyle (Seaking, Poliwhirl)
- **Items:** Nugget (southern field, requires Surf/Cut), Calcium (northern field, requires Cut), Super Rod (Fishing Guru's house).
- **Available Pokémon:** Tentacool, Quagsire, fishing varieties.

*Use this guide to navigate through Routes 12 to 15 efficiently, battling trainers and capturing Pokémon.*
