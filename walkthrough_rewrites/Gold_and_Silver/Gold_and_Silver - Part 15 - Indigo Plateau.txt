### Part 15: Indigo Plateau Walkthrough

**Indigo Plateau Overview**  
Indigo Plateau is the Pokémon League's hub for the Johto and Kanto regions. After defeating all eight Gym Leaders, trainers challenge the Elite Four and the Champion. A Pokémon Center and Poké Mart are available for preparation. Bring strong Pokémon and plenty of healing items.

**Poké Mart Items**  
- Ultra Ball: $1200  
- Max Repel: $700  
- Hyper Potion: $1200  
- Max Potion: $2500  
- Full Restore: $3000  
- Revive: $1500  
- Full Heal: $600  

---

**Elite Four Battles**  

1. **Battle 1: Will (Psychic)**  
   - **Strategy**: Use Bug, Dark, or Ghost attacks.  
   - **Key Pokémon**:  
     - Xatu (Lv. 40, Psychic)  
     - Exeggutor (Lv. 41, Grass)  
     - Slowbro (Lv. 41, Water)  
     - Jynx (Lv. 41, Ice)  
     - Xatu (Lv. 42)  

2. **Battle 2: Koga (Poison)**  
   - **Strategy**: Use Psychic and Fire moves. Watch out for Toxic.  
   - **Key Pokémon**:  
     - Ariados (Lv. 40)  
     - Venomoth (Lv. 41)  
     - Forretress (Lv. 43, Steel)  
     - Muk (Lv. 42)  
     - Crobat (Lv. 44)  

3. **Battle 3: Bruno (Fighting)**  
   - **Strategy**: Use Psychic and Flying moves.  
   - **Key Pokémon**:  
     - Hitmontop (Lv. 42)  
     - Hitmonlee (Lv. 42)  
     - Hitmonchan (Lv. 42)  
     - Onix (Lv. 43, Rock/Ground)  
     - Machamp (Lv. 46)  

4. **Battle 4: Karen (Dark)**  
   - **Strategy**: Use Bug and Fighting moves.  
   - **Key Pokémon**:  
     - Umbreon (Lv. 42)  
     - Vileplume (Lv. 42)  
     - Murkrow (Lv. 44)  
     - Gengar (Lv. 45)  
     - Houndoom (Lv. 47)  

---

**Champion Battle: Lance**  
- **Strategy**: Use Ice-type and Electric-type moves.  
- **Key Pokémon**:  
   - Gyarados (Lv. 44)  
   - Dragonite (Lv. 47)  
   - Charizard (Lv. 46)  
   - Aerodactyl (Lv. 46)  
   - Dragonite (Lv. 50)  

After defeating Lance, you will be inducted into the Hall of Fame, where your accomplishments are recorded.

**Final Notes**  
Prepare thoroughly before each battle, and make use of healing items to maximize your chances of victory. Good luck!
