**Part 21 Walkthrough: Pokémon Gold and Silver**

**Route 16**
- Connects Celadon City to Route 17. Use a Bicycle to navigate safely.
- **Wild Pokémon:** 
  - Fearow (Levels 27, 29)
  - Grimer (Levels 26, 28)
  - Muk (Level 30)
  - Murkrow (Level 28)
  - Slugma (Level 27)

**Route 17**
- Cycling Road; stopping will cause you to roll downhill.
- **Trainers:**
  - Riley: Weezing (Lv. 34) - Reward: $1088
  - Glenn: Koffing (Lv. 28), Magmar (Lv. 30), Weezing (Lv. 32) - Reward: $1024
  - Joel: 2x Magmar (Lv. 32) - Reward: $1024
  - Charles: Koffing (Lv. 30), Charmeleon (Lv. 30), Weezing (Lv. 30) - Reward: $960
- **Items:**
  - Max Ether (hidden in northern grass).
  - Max Elixir (west of four fenceposts).

**Route 18**
- Leads east to Fuchsia City.
- **Trainers:**
  - Bob: Noctowl (Lv. 34) - Reward: $816
  - Boris: Doduo (Lv. 30, 28), Dodrio (Lv. 32) - Reward: $768
- **Wild Pokémon:** 
  - Fearow (Levels 27, 29)
  - Grimer (Levels 26, 28)
  - Muk (Level 30)
  - Slugma (Level 27)

**Fuchsia City**
- Largest city in southern Kanto. Home to the Fuchsia Gym.
- **Wild Pokémon:**
  - Surfing: Magikarp (Level 10-24)
  - Fishing: Magikarp (Old Rod - Level 10; Good Rod - Level 20), Gyarados (Good Rod - Level 20)
- **Items:**
  - Burnt Berry (daily from a tree near the Poké Mart).
  - TM06 (Toxic) from Gym Leader Janine after defeating her.

**Fuchsia Gym**
- Leader: Janine (Poison-type specialist).
- Use Psychic, Electric, Ice, or Rock moves against her Pokémon.
- **Janine's Team:**
  - Crobat (Lv. 36)
  - 2x Weezing (Lv. 36)
  - Ariados (Lv. 33)
  - Venomoth (Lv. 39)
- **Rewards:** Soul Badge and TM06 (Toxic).

**Road Closed**
- The southern ocean route is blocked due to volcanic debris from Cinnabar Island. Exit Fuchsia City to Route 15. 

This guide provides essential directions, trainer battles, and item locations to progress effectively through these areas.
