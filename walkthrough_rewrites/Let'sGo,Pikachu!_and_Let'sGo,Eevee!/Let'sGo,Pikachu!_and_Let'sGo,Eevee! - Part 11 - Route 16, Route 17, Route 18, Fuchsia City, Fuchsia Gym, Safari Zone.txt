### Part 11 Walkthrough: Route 16 - Fuchsia City

**Route 16:**
- Links Celadon City to Route 17.
- **Trainers:**
  - **Punk Guy Lao:** Onix, Muk (Lv. 36)
  - **Roughneck Koji:** Raticate, Rhyhorn, Koffing (Lv. 36)
  - **Punk Guy Hideo:** Arbok, Fearow (Lv. 36)
  - **Camper Ruben:** 2 Psyduck (Lv. 35)

- **Available Pokémon:** Doduo, Pidgey, Rattata, Chansey (Grass 31-36)
- **Items:**
  - PP Up: North of tall grass (requires Cut)
  - TM14 (Fly): Northwest house (requires Cut)
  - Ultra Ball x30: Southern gate (after catching 40 species)
  - Awakening, Super Potion, Great Ball x3: Various locations.
  
- **Roadblock:** Wake Snorlax (Lv. 34) using the Poké Flute.

**Route 17:**
- Features flower-filled trails to Route 18.
- **Trainers:**
  - **Picnicker Dalia:** Vaporeon (Lv. 37)
  - **Beauty Alexis:** Jolteon (Lv. 37)
  - **Ace Trainer Johnson:** Wartortle, Magneton, Marowak (Lv. 39-40)

- **Available Pokémon:** Ponyta, Doduo, Eevee (Grass 33-38)
- **Items:**
  - Super Potion, Great Ball x3, TM58 (Drill Run) from Coach Trainer Grantley.

**Route 18:**
- Leads east to Fuchsia City.
- **Trainers:**
  - **Bird Keeper Wilton:** Fearow, Dodrio (Lv. 39)
  - **Bird Keeper Ramiro:** Pidgeot (Lv. 39)

- **Available Pokémon:** Pidgey, Rattata, Chansey (Grass 33-38)
- **Items:** Elixir (south of the gate), Silver Razz Berries (hidden).

**Fuchsia City:**
- Largest city in southern Kanto, home to the Safari Zone and Fuchsia Gym.
- **Pokémon Center:** Visit for healing and move tutoring.
- **Safari Zone Warden's House:** Return Gold Teeth to receive items and moves.
  
**Fuchsia Gym:**
- **Leader:** Koga (Poison-type).
- **Strategy:** Use Fire-, Flying-, or Psychic-type moves. Bring Antidotes for poisoning.
- **Trainers:**
  - Tamer Phil: Nidorina, Nidorino (Lv. 41)
  - Ace Trainer Kayden: Muk, Beedrill (Lv. 41-42)
  
- **Koga's Team:**
  - Weezing, Muk, Golbat, Venomoth (Lv. 43-44).
  
- **Reward:** Soul Badge and TM27 (Toxic).

**Safari Zone:**
- **Playful Park:** Interact with various Pokémon.
- **GO Park Complex:** Minigame to catch Pokémon imported from Pokémon GO.

**Next Steps:** Proceed to explore Routes 12-15 and Route 19 for further challenges.
