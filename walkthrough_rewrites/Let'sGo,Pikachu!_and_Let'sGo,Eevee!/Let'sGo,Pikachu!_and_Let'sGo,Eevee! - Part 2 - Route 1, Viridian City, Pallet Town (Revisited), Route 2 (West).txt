**Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee! Walkthrough - Part 2**

**Route 1:**
- **Overview:** Connects Pallet Town to Viridian City, featuring tall grass and ledges.
- **Wild Pokémon:** Pidgey, Rattata, Oddish, Bellsprout (levels 3-4).
- **Items:** 
  - Potion (from Viridian Poké Mart clerk after delivering Oak’s Parcel).
  - Razz Berry (found in bushes).

**Viridian City:**
- **Key Locations:**
  - **Pokémon Center:** Heals your team.
  - **Trainers' School:** Learn about status ailments.
  - **Poké Mart:** Stock up on items (Poké Balls, Potions, etc.).
  - **Parcel Delivery:** Receive Oak's Parcel from the clerk.
- **Rival Battle 1:** Battle Trace in Oak's Lab after delivering the Parcel. Use backup Pokémon for an easier win.

**Pallet Town:**
- **Oak's Lab:** Receive 20 Razz Berries and unlock Partner Play with your Pokémon.
- **Rival Battle 1:** Trace challenges you; use your best moves.

**Route 1 (Return):**
- **Free Sample:** Receive a Potion from the Poké Mart clerk.
- **Battle Youngster Ronny** for easy cash and experience.

**Viridian City (Second Visit):**
- **Rest:** Heal at the Pokémon Center.
- **Shop:** Purchase useful items, especially Antidotes for upcoming Bug-types.

**Route 22:**
- **Explore & Catch Pokémon:** Includes Rattata, Spearow, Nidoran♀, Nidoran♂ (levels 3-4).
- **Rival Battle 2:** Face Trace again; use previously effective strategies.

**Route 2 (West):**
- **Access:** Leads to Pewter City with Viridian Forest to explore.
- **Wild Pokémon:** Pidgey, Rattata, Oddish, Bellsprout, Caterpie, Weedle (levels 3-4).
  
**Next Steps:** Heal Pokémon and stock up on items before entering Viridian Forest.
