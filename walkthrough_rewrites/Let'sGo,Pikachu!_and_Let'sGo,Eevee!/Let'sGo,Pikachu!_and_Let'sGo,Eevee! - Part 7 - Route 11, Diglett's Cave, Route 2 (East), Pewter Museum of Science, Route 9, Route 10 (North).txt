### Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee! Walkthrough - Part 7

**Route 11**
- **Location**: Between Vermilion City and Route 12.
- **Key Trainers**: 
  - Coach Trainer Will (Reward: $2,100, Pokémon: Rattata)
  - Sailor Dirk (Reward: $1,080, Pokémon: Poliwag, Machop)
- **Available Pokémon**: Pidgey, Rattata, Drowzee, etc.
- **Items**: 
  - Super Potion (south side)
  - X Defense (middle)
  - Great Ball (south of the gate)
- **Notable Action**: Speak to the Scientist at the east gate to unlock the Judge function in your Pokédex after catching 30 species.

**Diglett's Cave**
- **Connects**: Route 11 to Route 2.
- **Available Pokémon**: Diglett, Zubat, Dugtrio.
  
**Route 2 (East)**
- **Items**: 
  - Great Ball (north of Diglett’s Cave)
  - Leaf Stone (south of the gate, needs Chop Down)
  - Light Up (from Oak’s aide after catching 10 species).
- **Key Encounter**: Meet Trace after learning Light Up; he can guide you to Cerulean City.

**Pewter City**
- **Pewter Museum of Science**: Use Chop Down to access the employee area. Speak to the Scientist to receive the Old Amber.

**Route 9**
- **Location**: Connects Cerulean City to Route 10.
- **Key Trainers**: 
  - Hiker Jeremy (Reward: $880, Pokémon: Onix)
  - Youngster Chris (Reward: $400, Pokémon: Diglett, Fearow).
- **Items**: 
  - Super Potions (southwest corner)
  - Ether (northwest corner).

**Route 10 (North)**
- **Connects Route 9 to Lavender Town**.
- **Key Trainers**: 
  - Team Rocket Grunt (Reward: $616, Pokémon: Raticate)
  - Coach Trainer Tasha (Reward: TM13, Pokémon: Poliwhirl, Primeape).
- **Items**: 
  - Super Lure (south of Route 9 intersection)
  - Great Ball (between cave entrance and Pokémon Center).
- **Ambush Event**: Team Rocket Grunts attack; Lorelei from the Elite Four intervenes, warning them and mentioning your Gym Badge quest.

### Summary
- Progress through Route 11, fight trainers, and gather items.
- Navigate Diglett's Cave to reach Route 2.
- Visit Pewter City for the Old Amber.
- Continue to Routes 9 and 10, defeating trainers and collecting items while preparing for an ambush by Team Rocket.
