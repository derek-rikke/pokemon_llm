**Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen Walkthrough - Part 12**

**Key Areas: Route 12, Route 13, Route 14, Route 15, Route 19, Route 20 (East), Seafoam Islands, Route 20 (West)**

### Route 12
- **Trainers**: 
  - Fisherman Ned: Goldeen (Lv. 22), Poliwag (Lv. 22)
  - Fisherman Chip: Tentacool (Lv. 24), Goldeen (Lv. 24)
  - Rocker Luca: Voltorb (Lv. 29), Electrode (Lv. 29)
- **Items**: 
  - TM27 (Return) from girl on the second floor of Lavender gate.
  - Super Rod from Fishing Guru's brother (south of Route 11).
- **Roadblock**: Use Poké Flute to wake Snorlax (Lv. 30) blocking the path.

### Route 13
- **Trainers**: 
  - Picnicker Alma: Goldeen (Lv. 28), Horsea (Lv. 28)
  - Bird Keeper Sebastian: Pidgey (Lv. 29), Pidgeotto (Lv. 29)
- **Available Pokémon**: Oddish, Bellsprout, Venonat, Pidgey.

### Route 14
- **Trainers**: 
  - Bird Keeper Carter: Pidgey (Lv. 28), Doduo (Lv. 28)
  - Biker Gerald: Koffing (Lv. 29), Muk (Lv. 29)
- **Items**: Pinap Berry (southeast corner), Zinc (in tall grass).

### Route 15
- **Trainers**: 
  - Picnicker Becky: Pikachu (Lv. 29), Raichu (Lv. 29)
  - Crush Kin Ron & Mya: Hitmonchan (Lv. 29), Hitmonlee (Lv. 29)
- **Items**: TM18 (Rain Dance) at the northern hill (requires Cut), Exp. Share from Professor Oak's aide after catching 50 Pokémon.

### Fuchsia City
- **Preparation**: Ensure you have Surf and Strength before heading to the southern islands.

### Route 19
- **Trainers**: 
  - Swimmer Richard: Tentacool (Lv. 30), Shellder (Lv. 30)
- **Available Pokémon**: Psyduck, Slowpoke (Fishing and Surfing).

### Route 20 (East)
- **Trainers**: 
  - Swimmer Barry: Shellder (Lv. 31), Cloyster (Lv. 31).
- **Available Pokémon**: Tentacool, Horsea (Fishing).

### Seafoam Islands
- **Exploration**: Navigate through currents and boulders to reach Articuno.
- **Articuno Battle Strategy**: Use Fire, Electric, and Rock-type moves to weaken it, then inflict status ailments like Sleep or Paralysis to increase capture chance.
- **Items**: 
  - Ice Heal (1F), Revive (B1F), Water Stone (B2F), Nugget (B3F, hidden).

### Route 20 (West)
- **Trainers**: 
  - Picnicker Irene: Tentacool (Lv. 30), Horsea (Lv. 30).
  - Swimmer Nora: Shellder (Lv. 30), Cloyster (Lv. 30).

This concise guide highlights the essential paths, trainers, items, and strategies for progression through the specified routes in Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen.
