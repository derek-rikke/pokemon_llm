### Concise Walkthrough: Part 8 - Rock Tunnel to Lavender Town

**Route 10 - Rock Tunnel**
- **Rock Tunnel** is dark; bring a Pokémon with **Flash**.
- **Trainers:**
  - **1F**: Defeat trainers like PokéManiac Ashton (Cubone, Lv. 23) and Hiker Lenny (Geodude, Lv. 19).
  - **B1F**: Face PokéManiac Winston (Slowpoke, Lv. 25) and others.
  
- **Available Pokémon**:
  - **1F**: Geodude, Zubat, Mankey, Machop, Onix.
  - **B1F**: Same as 1F, plus higher-level Machop and Onix.

- **Items**:
  - **1F**: Repel (Northeast), Escape Rope (West), Pearl (Southwest).
  - **B1F**: Revive (Southeast), Max Ether (Northwest).

- **Path**: 
  - Start at the **Northeast** for Repel, defeat trainers, go to B1F, collect items, battle trainers, and exit through **Southwest**.

**Route 10 (South)**
- Shorter than the north path. Trainers include Picnicker Carol (Pidgey, Lv. 21) and Hiker Clark (Geodude, Lv. 21).

- **Item**: Nanab Berry (east of Rock Tunnel exit).

**Lavender Town**
- Visit the **Name Rater** to change Pokémon nicknames for free.
- **Lavender Volunteer Pokémon House**: Talk to Mr. Fuji here.
  
- **Pokémon Tower**: 
  - Encounter **Rival Battle 5** on the second floor.
  - Ghosts can be battled after obtaining a certain item.
  
**Route 8**
- Connects Lavender Town to Saffron City. Trainers include Lass Julia (Clefairy, Lv. 22) and Gamer Rich (Growlithe, Lv. 24).

- **Items**: Leppa Berry, Lum Berry, Rawst Berry (hidden in tall grass, requires Cut).

**Underground Path**
- Use the **second Underground Path** to travel west to Route 7.

- **Items**: Various hidden items like Ice Heal and Antidote.

**Route 7**
- Trainers and Pokémon: Encounter Meowth, Pidgey, Oddish, Bellsprout, Vulpix.
- **Item**: Wepear Berry (southeast corner).

### Tips:
- Utilize **Flash** in Rock Tunnel for visibility.
- Heal your Pokémon before challenging trainers.
- Explore thoroughly for hidden items and Pokémon.
