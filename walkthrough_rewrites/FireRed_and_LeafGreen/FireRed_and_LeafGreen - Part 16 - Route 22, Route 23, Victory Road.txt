### Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen Walkthrough - Part 16

**Route 22:**
- Connects Viridian City to the Pokémon League.
- **Available Pokémon:** Rattata, Mankey, Spearow, Psyduck (surfing), Slowpoke (surfing), Magikarp, Poliwag, Gyarados (fishing), and others via rods.

**Rival Battle 7:**
- As you head west, Blue challenges you. He has a strong team, so exploit their weaknesses:
  - **Pidgeot (Lv. 47)**: Weak to Electric and Rock.
  - **Alakazam (Lv. 47)**: Weak to Bug, Ghost, and Dark.
  - **Rhyhorn (Lv. 45)**: Weak to Water, Grass, and Ice.
  - **Gyarados (Lv. 45)**: Weak to Electric and Rock.
  - **Exeggcute (Lv. 45)**: Weak to Bug, Fire, Flying, Ice, and Poison.
  - **Charizard (Lv. 53)**: Weak to Rock and Electric.

**Route 23 (South):**
- **Items:** 
  - Leppa Berry, Max Ether, Ultra Ball, Full Restore, Sitrus Berry, Lum Berry, Max Elixir (all hidden).
- Present your Badges to guards to enter Victory Road.

**Victory Road:**
- Requires **Strength** to move boulders onto pressure switches to progress.
- **Trainers include:** Cooltrainers, Black Belts, and Jugglers with levels around 41-48.
- **Available Pokémon (1F, 2F, 3F):** Onix, Machop, Geodude, Zubat, Arbok, Golbat, etc.

**Items in Victory Road:**
- **1F:** TM02 (Dragon Claw), Rare Candy, Full Restore, Ultra Ball.
- **2F:** TM37 (Sandstorm), Full Heal, TM07 (Hail), Guard Spec.
- **3F:** Max Revive, TM50 (Overheat).

**Navigating Victory Road:**
1. Move the first boulder to unlock the first area.
2. Collect items and battle trainers as you progress.
3. Solve boulder puzzles to access higher floors and defeat trainers.

**Route 23 (North):**
- Follow the path through the stone maze to reach Indigo Plateau.

This concludes Part 16 of the walkthrough. Prepare for the Pokémon League ahead!
