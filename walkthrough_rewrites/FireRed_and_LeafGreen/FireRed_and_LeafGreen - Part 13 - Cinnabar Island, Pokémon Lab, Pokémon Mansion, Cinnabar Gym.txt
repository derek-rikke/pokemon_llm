**Part 13: Cinnabar Island Walkthrough**

**Cinnabar Island Overview**
- Located between Routes 20 and 21 in Kanto.
- Key locations: Cinnabar Lab (revives fossils), Pokémon Mansion (contains items and trainers), Cinnabar Gym (fights Blaine).

**Available Pokémon**
- **Surfing**: Tentacool (Levels 5-40)
- **Fishing** (Old Rod): Magikarp (Level 5)
- **Fishing** (Good Rod): Horsea, Krabby (Levels 5-15)
- **Fishing** (Super Rod): Shellder, Staryu, Gyarados, etc. (Levels 15-35)
- **Fossils**: Revive Helix Fossil for Omanyte, Dome Fossil for Kabuto, Old Amber for Aerodactyl.

**Cinnabar Lab**
1. **Meeting Room**: Trade Electrode for Raichu or Tangela for Venonat.
2. **R&D Room**: Move Tutor teaches Metronome.
3. **Testing Room**: Trade Seel for Ponyta.

**Pokémon Mansion**
- Explore for hidden switches and journal entries.
- Key Trainers: 
  - Youngster Johnson (Ekans, Raticate)
  - Burglar Arnie (Charmander, Charmeleon)
- Items: 
  - Moon Stone, Escape Rope, Protein, etc.
- Fossil Regeneration: Speak to the Scientist to revive fossils.

**Cinnabar Gym**
- **Leader**: Blaine (Fire-type).
- Prepare with Water, Ground, or Rock-type Pokémon.
- Answer quiz questions correctly to reach Blaine faster or battle trainers for XP.
  
**Blaine's Pokémon**:
- Growlithe (Lv. 42), Ponyta (Lv. 40), Rapidash (Lv. 42), Arcanine (Lv. 47).

**Post-Gym**
- Receive the Volcano Badge and TM38 (Fire Blast) from Blaine.
- Bill invites you to One Island; prepare before boarding the ferry.

**Next Steps**
- Set sail for the Sevii Islands after concluding activities on Cinnabar Island.
