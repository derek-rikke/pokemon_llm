## Part 17: Indigo Plateau Walkthrough

### Indigo Plateau Overview
Indigo Plateau is the Pokémon League's headquarters in Kanto. Prepare with a Pokémon Center and Poké Mart before facing the Elite Four. Bring strong Pokémon and plenty of healing items like Potions and Revives.

**Poké Mart Inventory:**
- Ultra Ball: $1200
- Great Ball: $600
- Full Restore: $3000
- Max Potion: $2500
- Revive: $1500
- Full Heal: $600
- Max Repel: $700

### Elite Four Battles

#### Battle 1: Lorelei
- **Type**: Ice/Water
- **Strategy**: Use Electric moves against her Water/Ice types.
- **Key Pokémon**: Dewgong (Lv. 52), Cloyster (Lv. 51), Slowbro (Lv. 52), Jynx (Lv. 54), Lapras (Lv. 54).

#### Battle 2: Bruno
- **Type**: Fighting/Rock
- **Strategy**: Use Flying and Psychic moves. Grass and Water moves are effective against his Onix.
- **Key Pokémon**: Onix (Lv. 51), Hitmonchan (Lv. 53), Hitmonlee (Lv. 53), Onix (Lv. 54), Machamp (Lv. 56).

#### Battle 3: Agatha
- **Type**: Ghost/Poison
- **Strategy**: Use Dark and Ghost moves. Ground and Psychic moves are effective.
- **Key Pokémon**: Gengar (Lv. 54), Golbat (Lv. 54), Haunter (Lv. 53), Arbok (Lv. 56), Gengar (Lv. 58).

#### Battle 4: Lance
- **Type**: Dragon/Flying
- **Strategy**: Use Ice moves against his Dragons and Electric moves against Gyarados.
- **Key Pokémon**: Gyarados (Lv. 56), Dragonair (Lv. 54), Dragonair (Lv. 54), Aerodactyl (Lv. 58), Dragonite (Lv. 60).

### Champion Battle: Blue
- **Strategy**: Use Electric moves against Pidgeot and Gyarados, Water moves against Rhydon, and Fire/Ice moves against Exeggutor. Use strong physical moves against Alakazam.
- **Key Pokémon**: Pidgeot (Lv. 59), Alakazam (Lv. 57), Rhydon (Lv. 59), Gyarados (Lv. 61), Exeggutor (Lv. 59), Charizard/Arcanine (Lv. 63).

### Hall of Fame
After defeating Blue, you will enter the Hall of Fame, where your achievements are recorded. The game will save, and credits will roll, but your adventure continues!

Prepare well and good luck!
