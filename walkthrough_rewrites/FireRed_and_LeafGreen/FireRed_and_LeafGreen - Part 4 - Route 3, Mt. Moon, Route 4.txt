## Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen Walkthrough: Part 4 

### Route 3
- **Overview**: Leads east from Pewter City into Route 4. 
- **Trainers**:
  - **Lass Janice**: 2x Pidgey (Lv. 9) - Reward: $144
  - **Bug Catcher Colton**: Caterpie (Lv. 10), Weedle (Lv. 10) - Reward: $120
  - **Youngster Ben**: Rattata (Lv. 11), Ekans (Lv. 11) - Reward: $176
  - **Lass Sally**: Rattata (Lv. 10), Nidoran♀ (Lv. 10) - Reward: $160
- **Available Pokémon**: Pidgey, Spearow, Nidoran (♂/♀), Jigglypuff, Mankey.
- **Items**: Oran Berry (hidden), located between ledges near Youngster Calvin.

### Route 4 (West)
- **Magikarp Salesman**: Available at the Pokémon Center for $500.
- **Available Pokémon**: Magikarp (gift, Lv. 5).

### Mt. Moon
- **Overview**: Contains Trainers and Pokémon like Zubat and Paras.
- **1F Items**:
  - TM09 (Bullet Seed), Parlyz Heal, Potion, Rare Candy, Escape Rope, Moon Stone.
- **B2F Items**:
  - Star Piece, TM46 (Thief), Ether, Moon Stone, Revive. 
- **Key Trainers**:
  - **Super Nerd Jovan**: Magnemite (Lv. 11), Voltorb (Lv. 11) - Reward: $264.
  - **Team Rocket Grunts**: Various Pokémon (Lv. 11-13) - Rewards vary.
- **Fossils**: Defeat Super Nerd Miguel to choose between Dome Fossil (Kabuto) and Helix Fossil (Omanyte).

### Route 4 (East)
- **Overview**: Descends towards Cerulean City.
- **Available Pokémon**: Rattata, Spearow, Ekans, Sandshrew, Mankey.
- **Items**: Great Ball (hidden), Razz Berry (hidden), TM05 (Roar).
- **Move Tutors**: 
  - Left Tutor: Mega Punch
  - Right Tutor: Mega Kick (one-time service).

Follow this path to progress efficiently through Route 3, Mt. Moon, and Route 4, collecting items and battling trainers along the way.
