### Concise Walkthrough: Vast Poni Canyon - Trial 7: The Captainless Trial

#### Overview
After completing the Grand Trial of Poni Island, proceed through Vast Poni Canyon towards the Altar of the Sunne/Moone. Expect tough battles against various trainers.

#### Key Locations:
1. **Canyon (Initial Area)**
   - Lillie gives you a **Max Revive** and heals your team.
   - Find a **Max Potion** near the fallen tree.

2. **Cave 1**
   - Battle **Veteran Harry** (Torkoal, Arcanine) and **Backpacker Perdy** (Whimsicott).
   - Use **Tauros Charge** to break rocks for an **Elixir** and **Max Potion**.
   - Face **Ace Duo Kent and Aimee** (Alolan Sandslash, Ninetales).

3. **Upper Canyon 1**
   - Collect a **Revive** in tall grass and an **X Attack** near the cliff.

4. **Cave 2 (North)**
   - Obtain a **Zygarde Cell** and battle **Hiker Zachary** (Archeops, Rampardos).

5. **Cave 2 (South)**
   - Fight **Veteran Lynn** (Starmie, Shiinotic, Talonflame) and collect an **Escape Rope**.

6. **Upper Canyon 2**
   - Continue to the wooden bridge where Lillie heals you.

7. **Cave 3 (Upper)**
   - Collect a **Zygarde Cell** and **Full Restore**.
   - Solve boulder puzzles to progress.

8. **Upper Canyon 4**
   - Meet **Mina**, who gives you **Fairium Z**.
   - Battle **Punk Girl Anna** and **Punk Guy Adam**.

#### Farthest Tunnel & The Captainless Trial
1. **Tunnel Entrance**
   - An ancient stone monument indicates a trial site.

2. **Trial Battles**
   - Face wild **Jangmo-o** and **Hakamo-o** before reaching the end of the tunnel.

3. **VS Totem Kommo-o**
   - **Totem Kommo-o** (Level 45) appears with allies Hakamo-o and Scizor.
   - Use **Fairy** moves for effectiveness; **Flying**, **Psychic**, **Ice**, and **Dragon** moves also work well.
   - Defeated, Totem Kommo-o drops **Dragonium Z**, enhancing Dragon-type moves to **Devastating Drake**.

#### Items to Collect
- **Max Revive**: Given by Lillie.
- **Max Potion**: Near cave entrance.
- **Elixir**: In Cave 1.
- **Zygarde Cells**: In Cave 2 (North, Upper) and Cave 3 (Upper).
- **Fairium Z**: From Mina.

#### Trainer Battles Summary
- **Veteran Harry**: Torkoal, Arcanine.
- **Backpacker Perdy**: Whimsicott.
- **Ace Duo Kent and Aimee**: Alolan Sandslash, Ninetales.
- **Hiker Zachary**: Archeops, Rampardos.
- **Veteran Lynn**: Starmie, Shiinotic, Talonflame.
- **Ace Trainer Junko**: Lilligant, Weavile.
- **Scientist Ikaika**: Muk, Magnezone.
- **Punk Girl Anna**: Persian, Honchkrow.
- **Punk Guy Adam**: Pangoro.
- **Ace Trainer Hiroshi**: Absol, Lapras.
- **Veteran Heather**: Stoutland, Klefki, Tsareena.
- **Veteran Eric**: Granbull, Cloyster, Golem, Gengar.

### Conclusion
After completing the Captainless Trial, you will be prepared to move on to the next part of your adventure at the Altar of the Sunne/Moone.
