### Part 16: Route 128 and Seafloor Cavern Walkthrough

**Route 128 Overview:**
- Located between Route 127 and Route 129, leading to Ever Grande City.
- Team Magma (Omega Ruby) or Team Aqua (Alpha Sapphire) is in the area, seeking the underwater cave Captain Stern discovered.

**Key Trainers:**
- **Ace Trainer Cornelius**: Golem (Lv. 39), Raichu (Lv. 39) - Reward: $2,340
- **Fisherman Wayne**: Wailmer (Lv. 37), Seadra (Lv. 37) - Reward: $1,184
- **Triathlete Isaiah**: Starmie (Lv. 36-49) - Rewards vary ($1,440 - $1,960)

**Available Pokémon:**
- **Surfing**: Tentacool, Pelipper, Tentacruel
- **Fishing**: Magikarp, Luvdisc, Wailmer (Good/Super Rod)

**Items:**
- **Heart Scale**: 3 hidden locations (requires Dive)
- **TMs**: TM55 (Scald) in Room 7, TM26 (Earthquake) in Room 9.

---

**Seafloor Cavern:**
- Accessible via an underwater cave in Route 128.

**Room Navigation:**
1. **Entrance**: Hidden Heart Scale in the southeast corner.
2. **Room 1**: Use Rock Smash to clear boulders; battle two Grunts.
3. **Room 2**: Defeat a Grunt; push a boulder into a hole.
4. **Room 3**: Hidden Escape Rope in the southeast; continue to the next room.
5. **Room 4**: Solve boulder puzzles to proceed.
6. **Room 7**: Ride currents to find TM55 (Scald).
7. **Room 9**: Collect TM26 (Earthquake) before heading down to Room 10.

---

**Final Battle (Room 10):**
- Face **Maxie (Omega Ruby)** or **Archie (Alpha Sapphire)**.
- **Maxie’s Team**: Mightyena (Lv. 41), Weezing (Lv. 41), Crobat (Lv. 41), Camerupt (Lv. 43).
- **Archie’s Team**: Mightyena (Lv. 41), Muk (Lv. 41), Crobat (Lv. 41), Sharpedo (Lv. 43).
- Defeat them to witness the awakening of **Groudon (OR)** or **Kyogre (AS)**, causing drastic weather changes in Hoenn.

---

**Aftermath:**
- The awakened Pokémon heads to Sootopolis City. Follow Steven to investigate the changes in weather conditions.

**Next Steps:** Proceed to Route 126 and Sootopolis City for further exploration.
