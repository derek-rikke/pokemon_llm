**Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire Walkthrough - Part 19**

**Routes 132, 133, and 134 Overview**
- Strong ocean currents make travel difficult; explore all islands.
  
**Key Trainers:**
- **Route 132:**
  - Swimmer Gilbert: Tentacruel (Lv. 37), Sharpedo (Lv. 37)
  - Black Belt Kiyo: Machamp (Lv. 40)
  - Fisherman Ronald: Gyarados (3x, Lv. 36)
  
- **Route 133:**
  - Fisherman River: Seadra (Lv. 39)
  - Bird Keeper Beck: Skarmory (Lv. 39)
  - Ace Trainer Warren: Lairon, Alakazam, Manectric (Lv. 38)
  
- **Route 134:**
  - Swimmer Jack: Staryu (Lv. 36), Sharpedo (Lv. 38)
  - Ace Trainer Elaine: Lairon (Lv. 38), Tropius (Lv. 40)

**Available Pokémon:**
- **Route 132:** Tentacool, Pelipper, Tentacruel
- **Route 133:** Tentacool, Pelipper, Seadra
- **Route 134:** Tentacool, Wingull, Pelipper

**Items Locations:**
- **Route 132:** Rare Candy, Protein, TM34 (Sludge Wave)
- **Route 133:** Big Pearl, Max Revive, Calcium, Star Piece
- **Route 134:** Carbos, hidden Heart Scales, TM80 (Rock Slide)

**Exploration Paths:**
1. **Northern Path:** 
   - Start at Route 132, surf to the northwest island to find Rare Candy.
   - Continue to Route 133 for a Star Piece and TM77 (Psych Up).
   - Reach Route 134 for Carbos and Heart Scales.

2. **Central Path:** 
   - Surf to a Protein on a small island, then head to Route 133 for a Big Pearl.
   - Battle Trainers and collect items as you progress.

3. **Southern Path:** 
   - Fight Kiyo, obtain TM34, and collect Max Revive on Route 133.

**Sealed Chamber:**
- **Entrance:** Dive at Route 134 to find the chamber. Use the message on the north wall to access the next area.
- **Back Room:** Follow instructions involving Relicanth and Wailord to unlock the legendary Pokémon encounters.

**Island Cave (Route 105):**
- **Entrance:** Wait by the north wall for two minutes to find a hidden passage.
- **Back Room:** Encounter Regice (Lv. 40).

**Desert Ruins (Route 111):**
- **Entrance:** Follow the instructions on the north wall to reveal a passage.
- **Back Room:** Encounter Regirock (Lv. 40).

**Ancient Tomb (Route 120):**
- **Entrance:** Use Fly from the center of the cave to reveal the passage.
- **Back Room:** Encounter Registeel (Lv. 40).

**Meeting Regigigas:**
- Ensure all three legendary giants (Regice, Regirock, Registeel) are in your party, with Regice holding a cold item.
- Visit Route 105 during the day to encounter Regigigas (Lv. 50) in the Island Cave.

This concise guide covers essential trainers, items, and locations for your journey through Routes 132, 133, and 134, along with encounter details for legendary Pokémon.
