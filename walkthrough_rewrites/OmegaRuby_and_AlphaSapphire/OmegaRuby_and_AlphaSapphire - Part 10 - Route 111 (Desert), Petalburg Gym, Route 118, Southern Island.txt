# Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire Walkthrough: Part 10

## Route 111 (Desert)
- **Go-Goggles** allow access to the desert.
- **Treasure Locations:**
  - **Stardust** ×2: Hidden northeast of the southern entrance.
  - **Root/Claw Fossil**: Northeast corner (choose one, the other sinks).
  - **PP Up, Protein, Revive**: Various hidden spots.
  - **TM37 (Sandstorm)**: Southernmost point.
  
- **Trainers**:
  - **Camper Cliff**: Sandslash (Lv. 26)
  - **Ruin Maniac Dusty**: Baltoy (Lv. 24), rematch after badges.

## Back to Petalburg City
- Return options:
  - Backtrack through Mt. Chimney or head south to Mauville, then west to Verdanturf.
- Revive Fossil Pokémon at **Devon Corporation** in Rustboro.

## Petalburg City
### Petalburg Gym
- **Leader**: Norman (Normal-type).
- **Strategy**: Use Fighting-type Pokémon. Defeat at least 3 Gym Trainers.
- **Norman’s Pokémon**:
  - Slaking (Lv. 28), Vigoroth (Lv. 28), Slaking (Lv. 30). Use Protect against Vigoroth and capitalize on Slaking's Truant ability.

- **Rewards**:
  - **Balance Badge**: Obedience for Pokémon up to Level 60, ability to use Surf.
  - **TM67 (Retaliate)**.

## Route 118
- **New Pokémon**: Electric-types valuable for upcoming Gym.
- **Trainers**:
  - Various trainers including Aroma Lady Rose and Fisherman Barny (Carvanha, Gyarados).
  
- **Items**:
  - **Heart Scale**: Hidden on the beach.
  - **Good Rod**: From a fisherman.

### Key Events
- **Surfing**: Use Surf to cross bodies of water.
- **Gabby and Ty**: Battle them for a Double Battle challenge.
- **Meet Steven**: Encounter legendary Pokémon Latias/Latios and prepare for Southern Island.

## Southern Island
- **Legendary Pokémon**: Latias (OR) or Latios (AS) available.
- **Items**:
  - **Mega Bracelet**: From Steven after defeating Team Magma/Aqua.
  - **Latiosite/Latiasite**: Held by the respective legendary Pokémon.
  
### Battles
- **Team Magma/Aqua Admins**: Engage in a Multi Battle alongside Steven.
  
### Aftermath
- Team Magma/Aqua will flee, and you get to catch the legendary Pokémon. Steven gives you the **Mega Bracelet** allowing Mega Evolution.

To continue your journey, head back to **Route 118**. If you have an **Eon Ticket**, you can catch the other Eon Pokémon.
