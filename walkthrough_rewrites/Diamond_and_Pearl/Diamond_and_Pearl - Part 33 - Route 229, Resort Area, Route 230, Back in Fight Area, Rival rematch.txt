**Back in Fight Area:**

**Rival Rematch:**
- **Preparation:** Heal Pokémon, level 60+ recommended.
- **Location:** Barry waits at the Battle Park on weekends. 
- **Team Strength:** Barry’s Pokémon are now around Lv. 60, making this battle challenging.

**Important Notes on Barry’s Team:**
- **Initial Rematch:**
  - Staraptor (Lv. 58), Heracross (Lv. 60), Snorlax (Lv. 60), Roserade (Lv. 59), Floatzel (Lv. 59), Infernape (Lv. 64)

- **Hardest Challenge:**
  - If you defeat the Elite Four and Champion 20 times, Barry's Pokémon level up to 70, with tougher moves. Recommended level: 70+.
  - Reward: $7400

**Afterwards:**
