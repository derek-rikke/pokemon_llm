Game: Crystal
Part: 26
Keywords: Route 21, Cinnabar Island, Route 20, Cinnabar Gym, Route 19

### Part 26 Walkthrough

#### Route 21
- **Location**: Connects Pallet Town to Cinnabar Island.
- **Trainers**: 
  - Swimmer Nikki: 3x Seel (Lv. 28), Dewgong (Lv. 28).
  - Fisher Arnold: Tentacruel (Lv. 34).
  - Swimmer Seth: Quagsire (Lv. 29, 32), Octillery (Lv. 29).
- **Pokémon Available**: 
  - Surfing: Tentacool (Lv. 30-39), Tentacruel (Lv. 35-39).
  - Fishing (Old Rod): Magikarp (Lv. 10), Tentacool (Lv. 10).
  - Fishing (Good Rod): Chinchou (Lv. 20), Shellder (Lv. 20).
  - Fishing (Super Rod): Lanturn (Lv. 40), Kingler (Lv. 40).

#### Cinnabar Island
- **Overview**: Island with a Pokémon Center; Cinnabar Gym relocated to Seafoam Islands.
- **Item**: Rare Candy (hidden on the north side).
- **Nature's Madness**: Destruction from the volcanic eruption; only the Pokémon Center is rebuilt.
- **Final Gym Leader**: Blue, located at Viridian Gym after speaking to him.

#### Route 20
- **Location**: East of Route 20 leads to Route 19.
- **Trainers**: 
  - Swimmer Cameron: Marill (Lv. 34).
  - Swimmer Lori: 2x Starmie (Lv. 32).
  - Swimmer Nicole: 2x Marill (Lv. 29), Lapras (Lv. 32).
  
#### Seafoam Islands
- **Overview**: Houses the relocated Cinnabar Gym.
  
#### Cinnabar Gym
- **Leader**: Blaine.
- **Pokémon**: 
  - Magcargo (Lv. 45): Use special moves, especially Water-type.
  - Magmar (Lv. 45): ThunderPunch (Electric) for Water-type counter.
  - Rapidash (Lv. 50): High Speed; be cautious of Fire Blast.
- **Reward**: Volcano Badge ($5000).

#### Route 19
- **Location**: North from Route 20 to Fuchsia City.
- **Trainers**: 
  - Swimmer Harold: Remoraid (Lv. 32), Seadra (Lv. 30).
  - Swimmer Tucker: Shellder (Lv. 30), Cloyster (Lv. 34).
  - Swimmer Dawn: Seaking (Lv. 34).
  
#### Cleanup Complete
- **Note**: Volcanic debris cleared, allowing free movement between Fuchsia City and Route 19.
