### Part 28: Mt. Silver Walkthrough

**Location Overview:**
Mt. Silver is a challenging area between Johto and Kanto, home to powerful Pokémon. 

---

#### Pokémon Encounters

**1F Pokémon (Levels 42-46):**
- **Golbat** (5% - 45)
- **Graveler** (30% - 43)
- **Onix** (20% - 42)
- **Magmar** (10% - 45)
- **Ursaring** (30% - 44)
- **Larvitar** (5% - 15, 20)

**2F Pokémon (Levels 46-48):**
- **Golbat** (30% - 48)
- **Machoke** (30% - 48)
- **Parasect** (15% - 46, 48)
- **Ursaring** (20% - 47)

**Summit Pokémon (Levels 49-51):**
- **Golbat** (30% - 51)
- **Graveler** (20% - 48)
- **Onix** (30% - 48)
- **Ursaring** (10% - 50)
- **Larvitar** (9% - 15, 20)

---

#### Item Locations

**1F:**
- **Protein:** Southeast corner
- **Escape Rope:** Southeast between ledges
- **Ultra Ball:** Central area
- **Max Elixer:** Western ridge

**2F:**
- **Max Potion:** South-central area
- **PP Up:** Requires Surf/Waterfall
- **Max Revive:** Dead-end chamber (requires Surf/Waterfall)
- **Calcium:** Northeast ridge (requires Surf/Waterfall)
- **Full Restore:** Upper dead-end chamber (requires Surf/Waterfall)

---

#### Battle Strategy: VS Red

Red leads with **Pikachu** (Lv. 81). Use Ground-type moves like **Earthquake**. Follow up with these strategies for his team:
- **Espeon (Lv. 73):** Use Bug, Ghost, or Dark moves.
- **Snorlax (Lv. 75):** Use Fighting-type moves.
- **Venusaur (Lv. 77):** Use Fire or Ice moves.
- **Charizard (Lv. 77):** Use Rock-type moves.
- **Blastoise (Lv. 77):** Use Grass or Electric moves.

**Post-Battle:** After defeating Red, he will disappear. To encounter him again, defeat the Elite Four and Champion. Completing your Pokédex is your next goal!

**Reward:** $7700
