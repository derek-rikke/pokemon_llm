**Part 13: Blackthorn City and Surroundings**

**Blackthorn City Overview:**
- Located northeast of Johto, near the Ice Path and Route 45.
- Home to the Dragon's Den; access requires Gym Leader Clair's permission.

**Key Locations:**
- **Trade Pokémon:** In the eastern house, trade a female Dragonair for a Dodrio.
- **Move Deleter:** Located in the southern house; removes HM moves.

**Blackthorn Gym:**
- Leader: Clair (Dragon-type).
- Recommended: Use Ice-type moves against her team of three Dragonair and one Kingdra.
- Gym Strategy: Navigate the gym by moving boulders using Strength. Defeat trainers to progress.
- After defeating Clair, complete the Dragon user challenge at the Dragon's Den to earn the Rising Badge.

**Dragon's Den:**
- Enter via the lake behind the Gym (requires Surf and Whirlpool).
- Inside, face Cooltrainer Darin, collect items (Calcium, Max Elixir, Dragon Fang), and complete the elder’s quiz to receive TM24 (Dragon Breath) and a Dratini.

**Route 45:**
- Connects to Dark Cave and Route 46.
- Trainers: Hiker Erik, Cooltrainer Ryan, etc. Defeat them for cash and experience.
- Items include Elixir, Max Potion, and Nugget scattered throughout the area.

**Dark Cave:**
- Access from Route 45 and 46; requires Surf, Strength, and Rock Smash.
- Navigate through chambers to find various Pokémon (Zubat, Geodude, etc.) and items (Revive, TM13).

**Route 46:**
- Connects to Route 45 and 29; features Berry trees.
- Trainers: Camper Ted, Picnicker Erin, Hiker Bailey.
- Items: PRZCureBerry, Calcium, and X Speed can be found here.

**Important Notes:**
- Defeat Hiker Parry for Marill swarm alerts.
- Complete quests and battles to prepare for the Pokémon League. 

**Next Steps:**
- After earning all eight Johto Badges, head to the Pokémon League at Indigo Plateau.
