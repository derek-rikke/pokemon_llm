Game: Crystal
Part: 27
Keywords: Viridian Gym, Professor Oak's Lab, Route 22, Route 28

**Part 27: Viridian City to Mt. Silver**

**Viridian City: Viridian Gym**
- **Leader:** Blue (No type specialty)
- **Strategy:** Use Electric moves against Pidgeot and Gyarados; Rock moves against Arcanine. Bug, Ghost, and Dark moves work well on Alakazam and Exeggutor. Water attacks are effective against Rhydon and Arcanine.
- **Rewards:** Earth Badge, $5800

**Pallet Town: Oak's Lab**
- After earning all Kanto Badges, visit Professor Oak to gain access to Mt. Silver.

**Route 22**
- Connects Viridian City to the Pokémon League Reception Gate.
- Available Pokémon: Rattata, Spearow, Fearow, Poliwag, Ponyta, Doduo (varied levels and locations).

**Pokémon League Reception Gate**
- Access to Mt. Silver is now open.

**Route 28**
- Leads west from the Pokémon League Reception Gate into Johto.
- Available Pokémon: Ponyta, Tangela, Rapidash, Arbok, Doduo, Poliwhirl (varied levels and locations).
- **Items:** TM47 (Steel Wing) from a woman in a house (requires Cut); Rare Candy hidden to the east (requires Cut).

**Mt. Silver: Exterior**
- Last chance to prepare before climbing.
- **Requirements:** Surf and Waterfall for full exploration; Flash recommended for navigating the dark caves.
- Available Pokémon: Arbok, Golbat, Poliwhirl, Doduo, Ponyta, Rapidash, Tangela (varied levels and locations).

**Items in Mt. Silver:**
- Full Restore hidden on the southwest hill (requires Surf).

This concludes the walkthrough for Part 27.
