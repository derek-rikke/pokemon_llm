**Part 25: Viridian City, Route 1, Pallet Town**

**Viridian City**
- **Location**: Between Route 2 (north), Route 1 (south), and Route 22 (west).
- **Available Pokémon**:
  - **Surfing**: Poliwag (Lv. 5-14), Poliwhirl (Lv. 10-14)
  - **Fishing**: Magikarp (Old Rod Lv. 10), Poliwag (Old Rod Lv. 10, Good Rod Lv. 20), Poliwhirl (Super Rod Lv. 40)
- **Items**: TM42 (Dream Eater) from the sleeping man (requires Cut or Surf).

**Viridian Gym**
- **Status**: Open, but the Gym Leader is often absent. 

**Trainer House**
- **Battle**: Fight Cal (Lv. 50 Meganium, Typhlosion, Feraligatr). Only one battle per day.

**Route 1**
- **Trainers**: 
  - **Danny**: Jynx, Electabuzz, Magmar (Reward: $992)
  - **Quinn**: Ivysaur, Starmie (Reward: $1824)
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - Pidgey (Lv. 2-4), Rattata (Lv. 2), Sentret (Lv. 3)
- **Items**: Bitter Berry (daily from a tree south of the northwestern ledge).

**Pallet Town**
- **Location**: Between Route 1 and Route 21.
- **Available Pokémon**: 
  - **Surfing**: Tentacool (Lv. 30-39), Tentacruel (Lv. 35-39)
  - **Fishing**: Magikarp (Old Rod), Chinchou, Shellder (Good Rod)
- **Red's House**: Red is away training.
- **Blue's House**: Blue is serving as the Viridian Gym Leader.
- **Spa Day with Daisy**: Speak to Daisy between 3:00 PM and 4:00 PM for Pokémon grooming (once daily).
- **Professor Oak's Lab**: Rate your Pokédex; visit again after getting all 8 Gym Badges for a reward.
