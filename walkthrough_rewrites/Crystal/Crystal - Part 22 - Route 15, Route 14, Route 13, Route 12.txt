Game: Crystal
Part: 22
Keywords: Route 15, Route 14, Route 13, Route 12

### Part 22 Walkthrough Summary

#### Route 15
- **Location**: East of Fuchsia City.
- **Trainers**:
  - **Schoolboy Kipp**: Voltorb (Lv. 27), Magnemite (Lv. 27), Voltorb (Lv. 31), Magneton (Lv. 31) - Reward: $992.
  - **Schoolboy Tommy**: Xatu (Lv. 32), Alakazam (Lv. 34) - Reward: $1088.
  - **Teacher Hillary**: Aipom (Lv. 32), Cubone (Lv. 36) - Reward: $2592.
  - **Schoolboy Billy**: Paras (Lv. 27), Ditto (Lv. 35) - Reward: $1120.
  - **Teacher Colette**: Clefairy (Lv. 36) - Reward: $2592.
  - **Schoolboy Johnny**: Bellsprout (Lv. 29), Weepinbell (Lv. 31), Victreebel (Lv. 33) - Reward: $1056.

- **Available Pokémon**:
  - Pidgeotto, Nidorina, Nidorino, Venonat, Venomoth, Chansey, Noctowl, Hoppip, Quagsire.
- **Item**: PP Up (requires Cut, west side of the hill).

#### Route 14
- **Location**: Coastal road leading to Route 13.
- **Trainers**:
  - **Bird Keeper Roy**: Fearow (Lv. 29, 35) - Reward: $840.
  - **Pokéfan Carter**: Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle (all Lv. 29) - Reward: $2320.
  - **Pokéfan Trevor**: Psyduck (Lv. 33) - Reward: $2640.

- **Available Pokémon**: Similar to Route 15, with added Hoppip and Skiploom.
- **Item**: Gold Berry (held by traded Aerodactyl).

#### Route 13
- **Location**: Narrow path towards Route 12.
- **Trainers**:
  - **Bird Keeper Bret**: Pidgeotto (Lv. 32), Fearow (Lv. 32) - Reward: $768.
  - **Pokéfan Joshua**: Pikachu (multiple, Lv. 23) - Reward: $1840.
  - **Pokéfan Alex**: Nidoking, Slowking, Seaking (all Lv. 29) - Reward: $2320.

- **Available Pokémon**: Similar to previous routes, plus Tentacool and Quagsire (surfing).
- **Items**: Hidden Calcium (east of Trainer Tips sign).

#### Route 12
- **Location**: Connects Route 13 and Lavender Town (Silence Bridge).
- **Trainers**:
  - **Fisher Barney**: Gyarados (Lv. 30) - Reward: $1200.
  - **Fisher Stephen**: Magikarp (Lv. 25), Qwilfish (Lv. 31) - Reward: $1240.
  - **Fisher Martin**: Remoraid (Lv. 32) - Reward: $1280.
  - **Fisher Kyle**: Seaking (Lv. 28), Poliwhirl (Lv. 31) - Reward: $1240.

- **Available Pokémon**: Similar to previous routes, with more fishing options.
- **Items**: 
  - Nugget (requires Surf, Cut).
  - Calcium (hidden in northern field).
  - Super Rod (received from Fishing Guru's brother).
  - Elixir (hidden in northeast platform, requires Surf).

### Key Strategies
- Utilize **Cut** to access hidden items and paths.
- Rematch trainers for higher-level Pokémon.
- Consider trading for Aerodactyl on Route 14.

This concise guide provides essential information for navigating Routes 12-15 in Pokémon Crystal.
