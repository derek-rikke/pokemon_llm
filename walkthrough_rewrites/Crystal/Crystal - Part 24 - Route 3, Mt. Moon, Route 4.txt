### Part 24: Route 3, Mt. Moon, Route 4

#### Route 3
- **Location**: East of Pewter City, grassy area with several trainers.
- **Trainers**:
  - Youngster Warren (Lv. 35 Fearow, $560)
  - Youngster Jimmy (Lv. 33 Raticate, $528; Lv. 33 Arbok)
  - Firebreather Otis (Lv. 29 Magmar, Lv. 32 Weezing, $1392)
  - Firebreather Burt (Lv. 32 Koffing, Lv. 32 Slugma, $1536)
- **Pokémon**: Rattata, Raticate, Spearow, Ekans, Arbok, Sandshrew, Clefairy, Zubat.

#### Mt. Moon
- **Description**: Caves with unstable tunnels; known for Moon Stones.
- **Pokémon**: Sandshrew, Clefairy, Zubat, Paras, Geodude.
  
##### A Familiar Face
- **Rival Battle 6 (Silver)**:
  - **Chikorita**: Faces Sneasel, Golbat, Magneton, Gengar, Alakazam, Typhlosion (all around Lv. 41-45).
  - **Cyndaquil**: Same Pokémon as above but with Feraligatr.
  - **Totodile**: Same Pokémon as above but with Meganium.
  - **Strategy**: Target weaknesses with super effective moves.
  
##### Mt. Moon Square
- **Items**:
  - **Moon Stone**: Found hidden in a rock (requires Rock Smash).
  - **Shop** (10 AM - 5:59 PM): Fresh Water ($200), Lemonade ($350), Soda Pop ($300), Repel ($350), Poké Doll ($1000).
- **Clefairy Dance**: Occurs Monday nights between 8 PM and 4 AM; use Rock Smash for a Moon Stone.

#### Route 4
- **Description**: One-way road to Cerulean City.
- **Trainers**:
  - Picnicker Hope (Lv. 34 Flaaffy, $680)
  - Bird Keeper Hank (Lv. 34 Pidgeot, $816)
  - Picnicker Sharon (Lv. 31 Furret, Lv. 33 Rapidash, $660)
  
- **Items**:
  - **Ultra Ball**: Hidden northwest hill.
  - **HP Up**: Northeast hill.

#### Available Pokémon
- **Route 4**: Rattata, Raticate, Spearow, Ekans, Arbok, Sandshrew, Clefairy, Zubat, Surfing Goldeen, Seaking.
