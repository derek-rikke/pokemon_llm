Game: Crystal
Part: 18
Keywords: Route 8, Lavender Town, Route 10, Rock Tunnel, Route 9, Power Plant

**Part 18 Walkthrough Summary**

**1. Route 8**
- Connects Saffron City and Lavender Town.
- Trainers to battle:
  - Biker Dwayne: Koffing (Lv. 27-30)
  - Biker Harris: Flareon (Lv. 34)
  - Super Nerd Sam: Grimer, Muk (Lv. 34)
  - Super Nerd Tom: Magnemite (Lv. 32)
- Available Pokémon: Pidgeotto, Jigglypuff, Meowth, Growlithe, Abra, Snubbull.
- Item: PRZCureBerry on a tree northwest of Lavender Town (daily).

**2. Lavender Town**
- Key locations:
  - **Kanto Radio Tower**: Tune in for programs; obtain EXPN Card for access.
  - **Soul House**: Home for departed Pokémon, managed by Mr. Fuji.
  - **Lavender Volunteer Pokémon House**: Cares for orphaned Pokémon.
  - **Name Rater**: Change Pokémon nicknames for free.

**3. Route 10 (South)**
- Connects Lavender Town to Route 9.
- Trainers to battle:
  - Pokéfan Robert: Quagsire (Lv. 33)
  - Hiker Jim: Machamp (Lv. 35)
- **Rock Tunnel**: Dark cave; consider using Flash for navigation.
- Available Pokémon: Zubat, Machop, Geodude, Cubone, Marowak. 
- Items: TM47 (Steel Wing), Elixir, hidden X items, Max Potion, PP Up, Revive, Iron.

**4. Route 9**
- Connects Route 10 to Cerulean City.
- Trainers to battle:
  - Picnicker Edna: Nidorina (Lv. 30), Raichu (Lv. 34)
  - Hiker Tim: Three Graveler (Lv. 31)
- Available Pokémon: Rattata, Raticate, Spearow, Fearow, Venonat, Marowak, and fishing options.

**5. Route 10 (North) - Kanto Power Plant**
- Speak to the manager about retrieving a stolen item.
- Available Pokémon: Raticate, Spearow, Fearow, Electabuzz.
- Items: Metal Coat (from traded Magneton), TM07 (Zap Cannon) after returning the stolen Machine Part.

**Key Gameplay Points**
- Engage trainers for experience and items.
- Explore locations for Pokémon and items.
- Use Surf to reach the Power Plant after hearing about the theft.
- Prepare for battles by leveling up and healing your Pokémon.
